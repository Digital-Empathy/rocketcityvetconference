What it actually is

Most vendor halls are job fairs. Ours isn’t.

Most veterinary conferences turn their vendor halls into job fairs. Recruiters set up tables, corporate chains hand out merch, and your associate comes back from lunch with three new business cards and an opening to consider.

Rocket City does not invite corporate chains or recruiting firms. The vendor hall is a working showcase for veterinarians who want to see what’s actually new in equipment, technology, and tools. The companies on the floor are here because they want to support independent practices, not pull people out of them.

Why sponsor

One day of conference.
Twelve months of visibility.

A sponsorship at most one-day events ends the day after the event. Your logo runs for a week, the lights go off, and the next year you start over.

At Rocket City, the conference is one day. The visibility is twelve months.

Every active sponsor appears on this site for the full year, not just on an event-day registration page. Attendees, prospective attendees, and the broader independent veterinary community see your name year-round, anchored to a conference whose audience is unusually well-defined: doctors and decision-makers from solo and small-practice independent hospitals, the segment that makes purchasing decisions based on word of mouth and trusted recommendations.

You also reach that audience without competing against recruiting noise. Because corporate chains and staffing firms are not in the room, your message lands without a recruiter at the next table trying to take the doctor you’re talking to.
